Truffle Chips

Description

Thin, pan-fried potatoes with a hint of truffle oil and sea salt. Do I need further explain why you should make these?

Ingredients

  • 3 Potatoes
  • Truffle oil
  • Sea salt

Steps

  1. Slice potatoes thinly on a cutting board
  2. Heat a large frying pan to medium
  3. Lightly cover the bottom of the pan with olive oil
  4. Add a tablespoon of truffle oil to the pan
  5. Fry potatoes until golden brown
  6. Remove potatoes and place on layered paper towels on a plate
  7. Season with sea salt
  8. Enjoy!
