Old Fashioned

Description

The Old Fashioned is a whiskey-based cocktail with a hint of sweetness. It is the perfect drink to warm your entire body from the inside during cold winter months. The Old Fashioned is quite similar to the manhattan, substituting sugar for the sweet vermouth.

Ingredients

  • Whiskey (bourbon or rye)
  • Simple syrup
  • Bitters
  • Orange (half moon)
  • Cherry (we recommend Luxardo)
  • Water
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Place double Old Fashioned glass on a flat surface
  2. Pour 2oz whiskey into the glass
  3. Pour 0.5oz simple syrup into the glass
  4. Add 3 dashes of bitters to the glass
  5. Add orange half moon into the glass, muddle
  6. Add ice to the glass
  7. Add a splash of water
  8. Stir until glass is frosty
  9. Add cherry to garnish
